## **MAJOR BARRIERS:**

### **1. Access Restrictions:**
- Most comprehensive sports databases are **paywalled** (ESPN, Sports-Reference, etc.)
- **Scraping is blocked** or legally prohibited
- **Public APIs** typically only provide **current season** data
- **Historical datasets** require special academic or institutional access

### **2. Data Format Issues:**
- **Play-by-play vs. game-level:** Many sources provide detailed play data but not clean game summaries
- **Consistency problems:** Different formats across years and sources
- **Data quality:** Missing games, inconsistent player names, team name changes

### **3. Technical Complexity:**
- **5GB+ datasets** require significant processing power
- **Data cleaning** for 25+ years is a massive undertaking
- **Statistical validation** requires sports analytics expertise
